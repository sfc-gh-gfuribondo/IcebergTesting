Snowflake Iceberg Enterprise Readiness & Performance POC1. Background & ContextThis document outlines the evaluation of Apache Icebergª within Snowflake, focusing on enterprise-grade requirements. Rather than testing external engine interoperability, this POC prioritizes Performance parity, Feature compatibility with native Snowflake tables, Robust Governance, and High Availability/Disaster Recovery (HA/DR) capabilities.The goal is to determine if Iceberg tables can meet the "UHG Standard" for production workloads while providing the benefits of an open table format, specifically evaluating the transition toward Iceberg v3.2. Objectives & Success Criteria2.1 Primary ObjectiveValidate that Snowflake-managed Iceberg tables provide a viable, enterprise-ready alternative to native tables by meeting specific benchmarks for performance, security, and operational resilience.2.2 Success CriteriaIDSuccess CriteriaMetric / TargetSC-01Performance ParityRead/write latency within <10-15% of native Snowflake tables.SC-02Feature Compatibility100% pass rate for GA features (Streams, Tasks, Dynamic Tables).SC-03Governance IntegrityHorizon policies (Masking, RLS) enforced with zero leakage.SC-04Resilience (HA/DR)Successful regional failover meeting RTO/RPO targets.SC-05Cost PredictabilityDocumented TCO comparison including metadata management credits.3.
